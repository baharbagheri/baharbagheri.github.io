01 / About

Architecture as foundation. Computation as method. Robotics as frontier.

Architecture is the foundation. Computation is the method. Robotics is the research frontier.

With an architectural foundation and a master’s degree in Computational Design from the University of Tehran, Bahar Bagheri is currently pursuing interdisciplinary research at Texas Tech University focused on robotic arms in construction.

Her work connects spatial design, code, fabrication logic, and automation to investigate new ways of thinking about construction systems. Rather than treating technology as a visual layer, she approaches computation as a design method for geometry, behavior, material logic, and robotic action.
